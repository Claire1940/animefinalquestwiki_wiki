<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Complete the Yamato Questline">

Unlocking the Mythic **Yamato** weapon requires completing a multi-part questline that tests your combat capabilities and resource management. After speaking with Vergil in the Cube Room, select the "Show Quest" option to begin. The quest requires you to complete five demanding objectives across World 2.

| Objective | Requirement | Best Farming Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cyborg Arm** | SS-Tier Cosmetic | Drops from Mahito or crafted in World 2 |
| **Defeat Eizen** | 1 Clear | Bring a high DPS build to avoid health regen |
| **Defeat Hakari** | 1 Clear | Dodge the ground slams and solar explosions |
| **Defeat Mahito** | 1 Clear | Stay out of the purple domain expansion area |
| **Dungeons** | 50 Clears | Farm the first dungeon (Summon Gate) for speed |

The most time-consuming part of the quest is obtaining the **Cyborg Arm** cosmetic. This SS-tier cosmetic drops from the Mahito boss or can be crafted using rare boss materials. Additionally, you must defeat three of World 2's hardest bosses: **Eizen**, **Hakari**, and **Mahito**. These fights require optimal gear setups and precise dodging, especially Mahito, who utilizes a purple bar blinding mechanic that slows your movement and locks your skills.

The final requirement is completing **50 dungeons**. To complete this passively and quickly, you should run the very first dungeon, **Summon Gate**, on Normal difficulty. A well-optimized build can clear Summon Gate in under three minutes, allowing you to finish all 50 runs in roughly two hours of active play.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Yamato Skill Showcase & Doppelganger 4th Move">

Once unlocked, the **Yamato** immediately claims its spot at the absolute top of the SSS-tier weapon meta. It is a rapid-cycling blade that allows you to weave seamless M1 basic attacks and skill combos with almost zero downtime. Its area-of-effect (AOE) coverage is vast enough to wipe entire waves, while its single-target burst melts high-difficulty raid bosses.

| Skill Name | Type | Damage Multiplier | Cooldown | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Judgment Cut** | Active | High AOE | 25 seconds | Rapid multi-cut slash in a circular area |
| **Void Slash** | Active | Medium AOE | 30 seconds | Linear dimension cut that hits multiple targets |
| **Uppercut Slash** | Active | Single Target | 15 seconds | Launches enemies into an air combo barrage |
| **Doppelganger** | Secret | 2.0x Base Buff | Passive | Summons a clone; requires Doppelganger Title |

By default, the Yamato only grants you access to three active skills and its ultimate awakening, **Devil Trigger**. When Devil Trigger is activated, your character transforms, unlocking two devastating ultimate moves: **Hell on Earth** and **Void Time**. These moves deal massive multi-hit damage and grant temporary invincibility frames (i-frames).

However, the true power of the Yamato lies in its secret fourth skill: **Doppelganger**. To unlock this ability, you must defeat the Vergil boss inside the Cube Room and obtain the **Doppelganger Title** (a rare 3% to 5% drop). Equipping this title above your head unlocks the fourth move, which summons a dark clone that mimics your attacks and permanently buffs your base damage by **two times (x2.0)**.

The **Doppelganger Title** is not just cosmetic. Equipping it unlocks the secret 4th skill, which summons a clone and instantly doubles your base damage output.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Vergil Boss Fight Strategy & Mechanics">

Defeating the Vergil boss in the Cube Room is required to farm the **Doppelganger Title** and unlock your fourth skill. This boss fight is highly mechanical and punishing for players who attempt to mindlessly button-mash.

Vergil utilizes rapid sword slashes, teleportation, and wide-range Judgment Cuts. During Phase 1, focus on landing quick basic attack strings and immediately dashing sideways (Q) to avoid his counter-attacks. Keeping your camera locked in **Shiftlock mode** (Ctrl) is essential for maintaining mobility.

When Vergil's health drops to 50%, he enters Phase 2 and activates his own **Devil Trigger**. His attack speed increases dramatically, and he begins spamming massive circular AOE slashes. During this phase, do not attempt to trade damage. Wait for him to finish his skill animations, use your **Time Stop gear** to freeze him, and then unleash your ultimate skills for a safe and clean clear.
