Endgame in Anime Final Quest revolves around Nightmare Dungeons and Raids. These modes introduce mechanics that can one-shot players who simply "spam" buttons. Mastering the **anime final quest noob to pro** transition requires learning boss patterns and grouping waves efficiently.

1.  **Wave Grouping**: Do not attack enemies as they spawn. Run in a circle to draw all mobs into a tight cluster, then use your weapon's AOE skill (usually Skill 2 or 3) to delete the entire wave at once.
2.  **Ranged Prioritization**: Always kill ranged enemies (archers/mages) first. Their chip damage adds up and can cancel your skill animations.
3.  **Boss I-Frames**: Bosses like the Golem have a ground slam. Use your Dash (Q) right as the red circle fills to take advantage of invincibility frames (I-frames).
4.  **Raid Ticket Usage**: Only use your Raid Tickets when you have a full team or a highly optimized solo build. Wasting tickets on failed runs significantly slows down your cosmetic crafting progress.
