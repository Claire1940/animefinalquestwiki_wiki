<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Reroll and Optimize Passives">

Rerolling for the perfect **anime final quest passives** is a process that requires patience and resource management. You cannot simply spam rolls and expect a Godly trait within minutes. Effective players use a systematic approach to ensure they don't run out of tokens before hitting a usable tier.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stockpile Passive Tokens">
    Do not start rerolling until you have at least 50-100 tokens. Attempting to roll with only 5-10 tokens often leaves your best unit with a "Common" passive, weakening your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Your Unit's Role">
    Determine if your unit is a Main DPS, a Buffer, or a Stunner. A "Range" passive is vital for a Stunner, while "DMG" is the only priority for a Main DPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Set a 'Safety' Threshold">
    If you roll an A-Tier passive like "Ace" or "Reaper," stop and evaluate. If you have few tokens left, keep the A-Tier rather than risking a drop back to Common.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Pity Systems">
    Check if the current event offers a passive pity system or increased rates for specific traits. The Mahito Update often features boosted rates for "Cursed" type passives.
  </Step>
</Steps>
