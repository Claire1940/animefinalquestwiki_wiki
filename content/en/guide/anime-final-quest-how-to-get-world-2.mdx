<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step World 1 Completion Path">

To successfully reach the requirements for World 2, follow this structured progression path. This ensures you are never under-leveled or under-geared for the challenges ahead.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Join the Official Group">
    Before starting your grind, join the official Anime Final Quest Roblox group. This is a hard requirement for redeeming codes like **SOULREAPER** and **APOLOGIZEFORTHEDELAY**, which provide the initial spins needed for a decent weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Summon Gate Grind">
    Start by clearing Summon Gate. Your goal here is to gather materials for the **Strongest's Cape**. You will need to defeat the Golem Boss multiple times on Hard mode to satisfy early quest requirements and collect Golem Armor Pieces.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Agris Questline">
    Speak to Fiji near the Agris statue in the HUB. This questline is mandatory for endgame players as it eventually unlocks the **Agris Ultimate Skill** and the **Agris Sword** cosmetic, which provides a massive +75 HP and +20% Crit Chance boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Weapon and Gear Optimization">
    Use your accumulated spins in the lobby. Aim for at least a Legendary weapon like the **Umbrella** or **One-Eyed Reaper**. For gear, the **Coyote Pistol** is excellent for solo clearing, while **Time Stop** is the meta choice for team-based World 2 progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final World 1 Push">
    Clear Summon Station 2 on Normal and then Hard difficulty. Once you can comfortably clear this dungeon, you have officially mastered the World 1 mechanics and are ready for the World 2 transition.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Avoid spending all your Runes on early-game Epic weapons. Save your Rune stacks for Mythic weapons like the **King of Curses (Evolved)** to maximize your Skill DMG bonuses.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait and Rune Optimization">

Runes are the most valuable currency when preparing for World 2. They allow you to reroll traits on your weapons, which can provide multiplicative damage bonuses. A weapon with a "C" tier trait will perform significantly worse than the same weapon with an "S" tier trait.

**Top Traits to Search For:**
- **Accuracy III**: Provides a **+15% Skill Damage** boost. This is the gold standard for weapons like King of Curses or Lawer.
- **Fortune**: Increases Gold and EXP gain by **15%**. Use this while grinding from Level 1 to 50, then swap to a damage trait.
- **Power III**: Increases M1 (basic attack) damage by **15%**. This pairs perfectly with the **Strongest's Cape**.

| Trait Name | Rarity | Effect | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Accuracy III** | Legendary | +15% Skill DMG | Nightmare Dungeon clearing |
| **Power III** | Legendary | +15% M1 DMG | Basic attack focused builds |
| **Fortune** | Epic | +15% Gold/EXP | Leveling and Gold farming |
| **Crit Chance** | Rare | +5% Crit Rate | Stacking with Agris Sword |
| **Juggernaut** | Common | +50 HP | Early game survivability |

<Callout type="info" title="Rune Strategy">
