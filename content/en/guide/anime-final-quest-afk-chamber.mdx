<Section icon={Settings} title="Optimizing Auto Clicker Layout">

The configuration of your click points determines whether your character stays active or gets stuck on a victory screen. For the **anime final quest afk chamber**, a three-point system is the most robust.

| Click Point | Screen Location | Repetitions | Primary Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Point 1** | M1 Attack Button | 5x | Constant damage output |
| **Point 2** | Lock-On Button | 1x | Keeps camera centered on targets |
| **Point 3** | Above Skill 1 | 1x | Triggers Awakening & Replays match |

**Detailed Point Logic:**
- **Point 1 (M1)**: Setting this to multiple repetitions ensures that even if there is slight lag, the attack command is registered. This keeps your character swinging continuously.
- **Point 2 (Lock-On)**: This is vital. Without lock-on, your character might drift or turn away from the mob spawn points, leading to zero damage dealt.
- **Point 3 (Awakening/Repeat)**: This point serves a dual purpose. It activates your transformation/awakening for a stat boost and, most importantly, clicks the "Repeat" button that appears in the same screen area after a match ends.

<Callout type="info" title="Rotation Control">
If your click points don't align correctly, install a **Rotation Control** app on your device. Force the screen into "Landscape" mode to prevent the UI from shifting during the AFK session.
</Callout>
