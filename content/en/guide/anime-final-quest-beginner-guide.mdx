### Essential Controls for Combat
Mastering the controls is the difference between a successful clear and a wasted run. The game uses a standard Roblox action set with specific keys assigned to your gear and ultimate abilities.

| Action | Keybind | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | M1 / Left Click | Standard melee or ranged combo. |
| **Skills 1-3** | 1, 2, 3 | Weapon-specific special abilities. |

<Section icon={Map} title="Dungeon Progression and Difficulty">

The heart of the **anime final quest beginner guide** lies in the dungeon system. Dungeons are divided into themed "Stations" based on various anime worlds. Each dungeon consists of multiple waves of enemies followed by a final boss encounter. Clearing these provides the Gold, Cash, and Runes needed to progress your account.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Always farm the highest difficulty you can clear in under 5 minutes. If a Nightmare run takes 15 minutes but a Hard run takes 3, the Hard run provides significantly better rewards per hour.
</Callout>

### Difficulty Tiers and Rewards
As you grow stronger, you will move through three primary difficulty levels. Each level increases enemy health and damage but scales the loot quality accordingly.

| Difficulty | Requirement | Primary Loot Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal** | Level 1+ | Basic Gold, Starter Materials, Rare Weapons. |
| **Hard** | Stable Rare/Epic | Increased Gold, Epic Gear, Quest Progress. |
| **Nightmare** | Legendary/Mythic | Max Gold, Mythic Drops, High Rune Chance. |

### The Farming Loop
1. **Enter Dungeon**: Select the highest unlocked Station.
2. **Clear Waves**: Use AOE (Area of Effect) skills to group enemies and delete them quickly.
3. **Defeat Boss**: Observe telegraphs and use your Dash (Q) to avoid heavy hits.
4. **Collect Loot**: Ensure you pick up all dropped materials for crafting.
5. **Upgrade**: Return to the Hub to spend your earned Gold on spins or Runes on traits.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Runes, Traits, and Build Optimization">

Once you have a solid weapon, the next step in this **anime final quest beginner guide** is optimizing your build using Runes. Runes allow you to roll for **Traits**, which are passive modifiers that can drastically increase your damage output or farming speed.

<Callout type="info" title="Rune Economy">
Runes are a limited resource. Do not use them on Rare or Epic weapons. Save your Runes for Legendary and Mythic weapons that you plan to use for at least 20-30 levels.
</Callout>

### Trait Tier List for Beginners
Not all traits are created equal. Depending on your current goal (leveling vs. bossing), you should look for specific modifiers.

| Trait | Effect | Priority | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Accuracy III** | +15% Skill DMG | **Highest** | Best for almost every endgame build. |
| **Fortune** | +15% Gold/EXP | **High** | Essential for early-game progression. |
| **Power III** | +15% M1 DMG | **Medium** | Good for weapons with fast basic attacks. |
| **Critical** | +5% Crit Rate | **Low** | Only useful if stacking with Agris Sword. |
| **Juggernaut** | +50 Health | **Niche** | Useful if you are struggling to survive. |

### Building for Synergy
A "Build" consists of your Weapon, Gear, Trait, and Accessories. For example, a **Skill Damage Build** would look like this:
- **Weapon**: King of Curses
- **Gear**: Time Stop
- **Trait**: Accuracy III
- **Accessory**: Divine Wheel (+1.6x Skill DMG)
- **Cosmetic**: Agris Sword (+20% Crit Chance)

  <Step num="2" title="Farming Summon Gate">

  </Step>
