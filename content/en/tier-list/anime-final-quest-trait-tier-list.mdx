<Section icon={Trophy} title="2026 Trait Rankings: S-Tier to C-Tier">

Understanding the tier separation is vital for resource management. Since trait runes can be scarce, knowing when to stop spinning is key to progression. Below is the detailed **anime final quest trait tier list** categorized by their effectiveness in 2026 endgame content.

### S-Tier: The Meta Dominators
These traits are the gold standard. If you roll these, lock them immediately and do not reroll unless you are aiming for a very specific niche build.

| Trait Name | Primary Effect | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Exalted** | Maximum DPS Multiplier | All Endgame Content / Boss Rush |
| **Superior** | High Skill Damage + Double Crit | Crit-heavy builds like Garrow |
| **Accuracy III** | +15% Skill Damage | Pure Skill Damage builds (Ichigo/Saber) |
| **Fortune** | +15% Gold and EXP | Farming materials and leveling up |

### A-Tier: Excellent Alternatives
These traits provide significant boosts that can carry you through most Nightmare-level dungeons. They are slightly less powerful than S-Tier but still highly effective.

| Trait Name | Primary Effect | Why it's A-Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unleashed** | Massive Cooldown Reduction | Spamming ultimates and high-tier skills |
| **Power III** | +15% M1 Damage | Sustained DPS for weapons with fast M1s |
| **Accuracy II** | +10% Skill Damage | Solid placeholder until Accuracy III |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Avoid using Fortune for Boss Rush or Nightmare progression. While it is great for farming, it provides zero combat benefits, making it much harder to survive high-damage waves.
</Callout>

### B-Tier and C-Tier: Situational & Weak
Traits in these tiers should generally be rerolled once you have a steady supply of runes. They offer minimal impact on your overall clear speed.

| Trait Name | Primary Effect | Ranking Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical Chance** | +5% Crit Strike Chance | Outclassed by pure damage multipliers |
| **True Awakening** | Faster Awakening Gauge | Only S-Tier for Sword Saint; B-Tier for others |
| **Juggernaut** | +50 Health | Health is better gained from accessories |
| **Accuracy I** | +5% Skill Damage | Minimal impact in the current 2026 meta |
