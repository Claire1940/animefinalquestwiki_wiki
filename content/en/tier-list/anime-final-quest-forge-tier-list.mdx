### Tier Breakdown

#### SS-Tier: Ability Master
The undisputed pinnacle of the forge system. Ability Master grants the player access to the Flash Step mechanic, allowing you to teleport short distances and completely bypass dangerous boss attack patterns. Additionally, it provides a passive cooldown reduction across all your skills, allowing you to cycle your abilities faster than any other passive in the game.

#### S-Tier: Fire, Nature, and Ice
- **Fire**: Embodying the philosophy that offense is the best defense, the Fire passive adds a powerful burn effect to all your attacks. It scales directly with your weapon's base damage, making it exceptionally deadly when paired with Mythic or Evolved weapons.
- **Nature**: If you are struggling to complete Nightmare runs solo, Nature is your savior. It restores a percentage of your health whenever you land a hit, allowing you to sustain through chip damage and focus on dealing damage.
- **Ice**: Following a massive balance patch, the Ice passive now freezes enemies solid and triggers a localized explosion. This makes it a top-tier choice for grouping up waves and clearing them with a single AOE skill.

#### A-Tier: Acid
The Acid passive applies a corrosive debuff that gradually eats away at enemy health and armor. While it excels in long, drawn-out boss fights like the Golem or Demon King, it is less efficient for fast wave clearing where enemies need to be burst down instantly.

#### B-Tier: Wind
Wind offers nice quality-of-life improvements by boosting your movement speed. However, because mobility can also be obtained through traits or cosmetics (such as the Golden Krampus Horns), dedicating your weapon's forge slot to Wind is generally considered a waste of potential DPS.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Forging">
Do not waste your rare ores and gold forging passives on Rare or Epic weapons. Save your resources until you obtain a high-tier Legendary or Mythic weapon that you plan to use long-term.
</Callout>
