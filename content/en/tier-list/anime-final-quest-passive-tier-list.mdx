Succeeding in the challenging dungeons of Anime Final Quest requires more than just rolling a high-rarity weapon. Balancing both systems is essential for maximizing your damage per second (DPS) and survivability. ### SS-Tier: Ability Master
Ability Master is the undisputed king of passives in the late game. Crafted using materials found in World 2 Infinite Mode, it provides a massive, continuous health regeneration effect. This passive is highly recommended for players attempting high-tier content like the Mahito fight, where survival is just as important as dealing damage.

Nature is the premier early-to-mid-game passive. It is easily crafted once you reach level 20 and start farming World 1 Infinite Mode. While its regeneration is weaker than Ability Master, it provides a crucial safety net for beginner players who are still learning boss attack patterns and dodging mechanics.

### The Meta Traits: Exalted, Superior, and Unleashed
Exalted and Superior are considered the absolute best traits in the game. The reason they dominate the meta is due to the **Double Critical** mechanic. The critical chance granted by the Superior trait is calculated separately from your character's base critical chance, allowing you to land double critical hits that can deal over a million damage when paired with top-tier weapons like Dark Saber. Unleashed is also incredibly strong, offering the highest potential damage multipliers in the game, provided you can maintain the required combat uptime.

### The Progression Traits: Accuracy III and Fortune
For players who are still farming and upgrading their gear, Accuracy III and Fortune are excellent alternatives. Accuracy III adds a flat +15% skill damage boost, which is essential for weapons that rely heavily on skill rotations rather than basic attacks (such as King of Curses). Fortune is the ultimate utility trait, boosting your Gold and EXP gains by 15%, making it highly efficient for farming spins and resources in early dungeons.

### Synergy Spotlight: The Double Crit Setup
Pairing the **Superior** trait with the **Cyborg Arm** cosmetic is the strongest offensive synergy in the game. The Cyborg Arm provides a massive boost to your base critical chance and skill damage, which is then multiplied exponentially when the Superior trait triggers its independent critical roll. This combination allows skill-heavy weapons like Dark Saber to bypass high-defense bosses entirely.

<Callout type="info" title="Buff Stacking Warning">
Avoid running the True Awakening trait if you are already using the Cyborg Arm cosmetic. The game does not stack these two effects; instead, it will only apply the stronger buff from the Cyborg Arm, making True Awakening a wasted trait slot.
