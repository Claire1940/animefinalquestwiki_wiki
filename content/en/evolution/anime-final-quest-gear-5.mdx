<Section icon={Map} title="How to Unlock Gear 5">

Unlocking the **anime final quest gear 5** evolution is a multi-step process that combines standard dungeon farming with a highly unique, lore-accurate raid mechanic. Instead of simply gathering materials and clicking a button, players must face off against the Kaido raid boss on Kaido Island and trigger a specific awakening event.

The table below outlines the exact material requirements and their respective drop locations before you can initiate the evolution:

| Required Item | Quantity | Source Location | Base Drop Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Straw Hat (Weapon) | 1 | Weapon Spins (Lobby) | 0.3% Normal / 1% Lucky |
| Straw Hat (Item) | 1 | School/Metro Domain (World 2) | 5.0% Drop Rate |
| Drums (Item) | 5 | School/Metro Domain (World 2) | 7.5% Drop Rate |
| Kaido Awakening | 1 | Kaido Island Raid Boss | 5.0% Chance on Defeat |

Follow these steps to complete the evolution process:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Base Straw Hat Weapon">
    Before starting the evolution grind, you must successfully spin the base Straw Hat weapon from the lobby. It is an Eternal-rarity weapon with a 0.3% drop rate from normal spins and a 1.0% drop rate from lucky spins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm World 2 Dungeons">
    Head to World 2 and repeatedly clear either the School Domain or the Metro Domain. You need to collect one Straw Hat drop and five Drum drops. Farming on Normal difficulty is recommended to maximize clear speeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Level 50 & Enter Kaido Island">
    Ensure your account is at least Level 50, which is the minimum level requirement to access the Kaido Island raid. Equip your base Straw Hat weapon before entering the raid lobby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger the Awakening Mechanic">
    Fight Kaido (who starts with 250,000 HP). Once his HP drops to 50,000, he stops taking damage and becomes invincible. At this point, you must let Kaido defeat you. Upon your defeat, there is a 5% chance you will awaken into Gear 5 to finish him off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalize the Evolution">
    Once you have successfully awakened during the raid and secured the World 2 items, return to the lobby. Open the weapon evolution menu, select the Straw Hat weapon, and click Evolve to permanently unlock Gear 5.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Awakening Caution">
Do not let Kaido defeat you before his HP drops to the 50,000 threshold. Dying early in the fight will result in a standard raid failure and will not trigger the 5% awakening check.
</Callout>

The true strength of the **anime final quest gear 5** evolution lies in its versatile moveset.
