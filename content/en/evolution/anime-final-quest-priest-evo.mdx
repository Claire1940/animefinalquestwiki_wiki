Evolving your weapon requires a trip to the Crafting Station in the HUB. The evolution process is not automatic; you must manually gather several rare boss drops, high-tier runes, and a significant amount of gold to complete the upgrade.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Base Priest Weapon">
    Obtain the base Priest weapon through Weapon Lucky Spins in the lobby. Ensure it is unequipped before attempting to evolve it at the crafting station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Hard Mode Summon Gate">
    Defeat the Golem Boss in the Summon Gate dungeon on Hard difficulty. You will need to collect multiple Golem's Hearts and Goblin Armor Pieces, which serve as core crafting components.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Runes and Gold">
    Accumulate at least 50 Runes through Nightmare dungeon clears or active codes. You will also need 150,000 Gold to cover the evolution fee.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visit the Crafting Station">
    Approach the Crafting Station in the main HUB, select the Priest weapon from your inventory, and confirm the evolution to unlock the evolved Priest form.
  </Step>
</Steps>
