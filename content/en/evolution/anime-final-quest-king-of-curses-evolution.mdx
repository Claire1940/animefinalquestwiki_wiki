<Section icon={Swords} title="Evolved Skill Kit and Damage Analysis">

Once the **anime final quest king of curses evolution** is complete, the weapon gains a sophisticated moveset that balances area-of-effect (AOE) clearing with high-impact single-target damage. The base evolved form (pre-awakening) is already sufficient for clearing most wave-based content.

The basic attacks receive a noticeable buff in speed and range. Each hit in the combo deals between **300 to 400 damage**, featuring a knockback effect that provides excellent crowd control. This makes the weapon viable even when your primary skills are on cooldown.

| Skill Name | Type | Damage Approx. | Cooldown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dismantle Cross** | Line / Multi-hit | 2,479 per hit (x2) | 40 Seconds |
| **Falling Light** | Large AOE | 4,000 | 30 Seconds |
| **Fuga (Fire Arrow)** | Medium Range | 5,000 | 60 Seconds |
| **Basic Combo** | Melee Slashing | 300 - 400 per hit | N/A |

<Callout type="tip" title="Skill Optimization">
Use **Fuga** when surrounded by heavy hitters. During the casting animation of the flaming arrow, your character becomes invulnerable to damage, allowing you to bypass dangerous boss mechanics while dealing 5,000 damage.
</Callout>

The **Dismantle Cross** skill is your primary tool for deleting waves. Because it hits twice in a straight line, positioning is key. You should aim to line up enemies to maximize the 5,000 total damage output. While the 40-second cooldown is lengthy, the sheer range makes it one of the most practical skills in the kit.
