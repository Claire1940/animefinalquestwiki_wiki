<Section icon={Shield} title="Rune Optimization and Trait Rankings">

Runes are the currency of optimization in Anime Final Quest. Spending them recklessly on low-tier weapons is the most common mistake beginners make. You should only use runes once you have secured a Legendary or Mythic weapon that fits your playstyle.

| Trait Name | Effect | Tier | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Accuracy III** | **+15% Skill DMG** | **S** | Endgame Nightmare clears |
| **Fortune** | **+15% Gold/EXP** | **S** | Mid-game resource farming |
| **Power III** | **+15% M1 DMG** | **A** | Fast basic attack builds |
| **Critical Chance** | **+5% Crit Rate** | **B** | Boss-killer configurations |
| **Juggernaut** | **+50 HP** | **C** | Early game survivability |

Traits should be viewed as multipliers. If your weapon has a high base skill damage, Accuracy III will provide a much higher effective DPS increase than Power III. Conversely, if you are using the Strongest's Cape accessory, which doubles M1 damage, Power III becomes significantly more valuable.

<Callout type="info" title="Rune Economy">
Do not chase "perfect" traits too early. A "Fortune" trait is often better during your first 50 levels than a "Power III" trait because it accelerates your ability to buy more spins and runes.
</Callout>
