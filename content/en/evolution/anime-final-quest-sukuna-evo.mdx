<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Weapon Upgrades and Priority Build">

The evolution update also introduces a robust Weapon Upgrade system. Fully maxing out every single stat on a weapon is a massive gold sink, costing approximately 4.25 million gold coins in total. Because gold is highly valuable, you must prioritize your stat upgrades carefully to maximize your combat efficiency early on.

The absolute highest priority for any build is **Skill Cooldown**. Reducing your cooldowns is not just about increasing your damage per second (DPS); it is also your primary defensive tool. In Anime Final Quest, activating skills grants your character temporary invincibility frames (I-frames). Cycling through your skills rapidly ensures that you remain virtually untouchable during chaotic boss fights.

After securing your cooldowns, focus on **Skill Damage** to increase your burst potential. Following that, invest in **Critical Chance** and **M1 Damage** depending on your specific playstyle. Speed is completely useless in the current meta and should always be your last priority.

<Callout type="success" title="Gold Saving Strategy">
Do not spread your gold upgrades across multiple low-tier weapons. Save all your gold coins specifically for Heian Sukuna or your primary meta weapon to avoid running out of funds.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="King's Vessel Weapon Showcase">

Before you unlock the Heian Sukuna evolution, you will spend a significant amount of time using the King's Vessel weapon. The basic attacks (M1) consist of wide, sweeping slashes with generous range. This makes clearing groups of minor enemies straightforward. The skill set offers a mix of auto-aim utility and high-area-of-effect (AoE) damage:

- **Piercing Blood**: A reliable, auto-aiming projectile skill. While its range is moderate, it automatically tracks targets, making it perfect for hitting mobile bosses.
- **Cleave**: The strongest standard skill in the kit. It features a massive AoE and high damage, making it the premier choice for wave skipping.
- **Divergent Slam**: A heavy-hitting strike featuring distinct black and red lightning effects. It has a slight wind-up time but deals massive damage upon impact.
- **Unleash (Ultimate)**: A devastating ultimate skill that deals upwards of 6,000 damage. It is best used when your other skills are on cooldown to maintain constant pressure.
