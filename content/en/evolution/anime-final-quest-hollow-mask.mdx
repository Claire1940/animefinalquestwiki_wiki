<Steps>

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Opening Salvo">
    Start the fight with Geta Strike to chip away health from a distance. Use Crimson Fang to close the gap once the boss uses his initial slam.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Managing Cooldowns">
    The boss has high HP but relatively slow recovery. Cycle through your base moves, prioritizing Heaven Fall Cleave whenever it is off cooldown.
  </Step>
