<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Unlock the Yamato Weapon">

Before you can even begin farming for the Doppelganger Title, you must first secure the Yamato weapon. This is a multi-step progression quest designed for high-level players. You must reach at least Level 55 to gain access to the Curse Realm and enter the Station Domain dungeon. Inside this dungeon, you have a 5% base chance to drop the Cube Room Portal, which is your key to Vergil's dimension.

Once you have the Cube Room Portal, use it from your inventory to teleport to the Cube Room where Vergil waits. Interacting with him allows you to accept his questline. This quest is notorious for being harder than the Soul Manipulator quest, as it requires highly rare drops from the Mahito boss.

| Quest Requirement | Detail | Source |
| **Level 55** | Minimum level required to enter the Station Domain | Character Progression |
| **Cube Room Portal** | Item used to teleport to Vergil's dimension | Station Domain (5% drop rate) |
| **Cyborg Arm** | Rare quest material dropped by Mahito | Mahito Boss |
| **Manipulator Hand** | Rare crafting component | Curse Realm Drops |

To defeat Mahito efficiently, you must prepare for a long and exhausting battle. Mahito's bladed hands deal massive physical damage, and his domain expansion can quickly blind you. When blinded, you cannot use any skills, and your movement speed is severely reduced. Focus on dodging his attacks sideways and saving your burst windows for when his domain ends.

<Section icon={Map} title="Farming the Doppelganger Title">

Once you have claimed the Yamato weapon, you must return to the Cube Room to fight the Yamato Boss (Vergil himself) to farm the Doppelganger Title. This title drops with a low 3% chance upon defeating the boss.

The fight against the Yamato Boss is a true test of mechanical skill. During his first phase, focus on building up your awakening meter as quickly as possible. When he transitions into phase two, he will heal a portion of his HP and begin using bladed hands and domain expansions. Do not get hit by his purple bar attacks, or you will be blinded and silenced.

| Boss Name | Drop Item | Drop Rate | Utility |
| **Yamato Boss** | Doppelganger Title | 3% | Unlocks the secret 4th move |
| **Yamato Boss** | Yamato Weapon | Quest Claim | Main weapon for the build |
| **Yamato Boss** | Raid Tokens | 100% | Used for Soul Manipulator |

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat Vergil in Battle">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm for the 3% Drop">

<Section icon={Target} title="Maximizing the Doppelganger Build">

Unlocking the 4th move is only half the battle; you must now optimize your build to make the most of the 2x damage multiplier. Since the Doppelganger skill doubles your base damage, any flat stat increases or damage multipliers from your traits and accessories will be amplified exponentially.

For traits, you should aim for either Superior or Accuracy III. Accuracy III provides a flat +15% increase to skill damage, which directly buffs all four of Yamato's moves. If you prefer a basic attack playstyle, Power III (+15% M1 Damage) is a viable alternative, especially when paired with the Strongest's Cape.

| Gear Piece | Best Option | Key Stat Multiplier |
| **Weapon Trait** | Superior / Accuracy III | +15% Skill Damage |
| **Accessory** | Agris Sword | +75 HP, +20% Crit Chance |
| **Cape** | Strongest's Cape | x2.0 M1 Damage |
| **Rune Focus** | Skill Damage / Crit | Multiplies clone burst |

For accessories, the Agris Sword is an absolute must-have. It grants +75 HP and a massive +20% Critical Chance. When combined with a Critical Chance trait, your Yamato slashes will consistently land critical hits, shredding through boss HP bars in seconds.
