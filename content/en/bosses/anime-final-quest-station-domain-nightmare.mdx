<Section icon={Target} title="Farming Route: Anime Final Quest Station Domain Nightmare Guide">

To maximize your efficiency, you should adopt the "Wave Skip Tech". This advanced strategy allows you to skip half of the enemies that would normally spawn in a dungeon wave, dramatically reducing clear times. By clearing waves in a single, coordinated burst, you prevent the game from spawning secondary waves, allowing you to complete dungeons in record time.

Implementing this strategy requires careful movement and timing. Follow these steps to optimize your farming route through Summon Station 2 and the School Domain.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather the Mob Wave">
    Begin the stage and wait for all enemies in the current wave to spawn. Look at the top-right counter to verify the spawn count. Walk in a tight circle around the spawn area to round up the mobs into a single, compact group.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Your Main AOE Skill">
    Once the enemies are tightly clustered together, activate your weapon's largest area-of-effect skill. Strong options include the King of Curses' Cleave or Broly's explosive blasts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Wave Skip">
    Ensure that every single enemy in the spawn group dies at the exact same moment. If successful, the game will immediately advance to the next wave, completely skipping the second wave of enemies that would normally appear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Repeat and Reset">
    Continue this cycle for Wave 2 and beyond. In School Domain Endless, reset your run around wave 150 to 200, as reward scaling plummets after this point and the time-to-clear ratio becomes inefficient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Mobility Boost">
Equipping the Anti-Mask cosmetic or the Ability Master passive is highly recommended for this route. The Flash Step capability allows you to dash through enemies safely, making mob grouping incredibly easy without taking chip damage.
</Callout>

</Section>
