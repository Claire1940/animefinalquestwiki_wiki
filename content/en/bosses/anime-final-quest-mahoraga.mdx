<Section icon={Target} title="Mahoraga Boss Mechanics and Phases">

The **anime final quest mahoraga** boss fight takes place in a stylized Shibuya environment. Unlike previous bosses, Mahoraga features a unique "taming" mechanic that mirrors the source material. The boss starts with 100,000 HP, similar to the Igris encounter, but the difficulty lies in its adaptive capabilities and high-damage slashes.

The fight is divided into two distinct phases, triggered by health thresholds and the Divine Wheel meter.

| Phase | HP Threshold | Key Mechanics | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | 100,000 - 20,000 | Rock throws, charging attacks, basic slashes | Moderate |
| **Phase 2** | 40,000 (Regen) | Enhanced Sukuna-style slashes, increased speed | High |
| **QTE** | Random Trigger | Spacebar spamming to deal ~1,000 bonus damage | Low |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
You are permitted only **one life** during this boss fight. If you fall, the run ends immediately. Avoid "death cycling" tactics used in lower-tier dungeons; focus entirely on sustainability and dodging.
</Callout>

During the encounter, a "Wheel Meter" will gradually fill. While its exact failure condition is still being debated by the community, it is widely believed that a full meter grants the boss massive damage resistance or triggers an enrage timer. Winning the Quick Time Events (QTE) is the most effective way to interrupt his momentum and deal consistent chunks of damage.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Evolution Guide">

Obtaining the Mahoraga evolution in **anime final quest mahoraga** requires patience and specific item drops. You cannot simply buy this evolution; you must prove your strength in the arena.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stockpile Resources">
    Ensure you have reached at least Level 100 to unlock the necessary titles and have approximately 15 million cash (Bruce Wayne title) to afford late-game gear upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip the Anti-Mask">
    The Anti-Mask is essential for this fight as it provides **Flash Step**. This movement ability is your primary tool for dodging Mahoraga's long-range slashes and rock throws.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm the Wheel Pieces">
    Enter the Divine General Evolution room. You need to collect **6 Wheel Pieces**. These have a **15% drop rate** per successful boss kill. Average run times should be between 3 and 5 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master the QTE">
    When the boss charges, a spacebar prompt will appear. Spamming this quickly deals roughly 1,000 damage and prevents the boss from landing a devastating hit. Never ignore these prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Evolution">
    Once all 6 pieces are collected, navigate to the evolution menu to unlock the Mahoraga form. This evolution is permanent and does not have a time limit for completion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Tip">
If you are struggling with the 15% drop rate, use the **Acid Passive**. The damage-over-time effect continues to tick even during the QTE animations, potentially shaving 20-30 seconds off your total clear time.
</Callout>
