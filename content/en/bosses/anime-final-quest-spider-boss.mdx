<Section icon={Swords} title="Spider Boss Attack Patterns and Mechanics">

The fight against the spider boss is highly dynamic, featuring a mix of projectile attacks, ground hazards, and minion summons. Understanding these mechanics is the difference between a clean run and an immediate defeat. The boss utilizes four primary abilities throughout the encounter, scaling in speed and aggression as its health pool decreases.

| Attack Name | Description | Counter Strategy | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Web Spit** | Shoots three web projectiles that slow players and disable Dash | Use the Umbrella weapon's iframe skill or dodge laterally | High |
| **Venom Puddle** | Spits venom that creates persistent poison zones on the floor | Move out of the green puddles immediately to avoid DOT | Medium |
| **Brood Hatch** | Summons five small spider minions to distract and chip health | Use King of Curses AOE skills to clear them instantly | Medium |
| **Enraged Slam** | A massive physical shockwave triggered below 30% health | Jump and Dash away from the boss when the red circle appears | Critical |

Managing your positioning during the Venom Puddle phase is critical. If you allow the boss to cover the center of the arena in poison, you will lose your safe zones, making it nearly impossible to dodge the Enraged Slam later in the fight.

<Callout type="tip" title="I-Frame Utilization">
The Legendary Umbrella weapon is highly valued in this fight due to its iframe capabilities. Activating its defensive skills right as the Web Spit is about to hit will completely negate the slow effect.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Strategy to Defeat the Anime Final Quest Spider Boss">

Executing the fight correctly requires patience and precise timing. Follow this step-by-step progression guide to systematically dismantle the **anime final quest spider boss** without taking unnecessary damage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement and Minion Control">
    Begin the fight by baiting the boss's initial Web Spit. Dash sideways immediately as the animation starts. Once the projectile misses, the boss will summon its first wave of spider minions. Do not focus on the boss yet; use your primary AOE skills to clear the minions instantly to prevent them from chipping your health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Kiting and Poison Management">
    As the boss begins spitting Venom Puddles, slowly circle the outer perimeter of the arena. Keep the center of the room clean. Use ranged attacks or quick M1 combos, but never commit to a full attack string unless you have a clear dash path open behind you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Burst Windows and Gear Activation">
    When the boss finishes its attack cycle, it will enter a brief recovery state. This is your window to activate your utility gear. If using Time Stop, freeze the boss and unleash your ultimate skills. If using the Solo DPS build, activate your Agris Awakening for a temporary power spike and unload your highest-damaging rotations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Surviving the Enraged Phase">
    Once the boss falls below 30% health, it will enter an enraged state, increasing its attack speed and utilizing the Enraged Slam. Prioritize survival over damage. Keep your distance, use your Dash (Q) to escape red warning zones, and rely on ranged skills or kiting to safely finish the encounter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Patience Wins">
During the enraged phase, greedy players often get caught in a Web Spit followed immediately by an Enraged Slam. Keep your cool, wait for the slam to miss, and then strike.
</Callout>

</Section>
